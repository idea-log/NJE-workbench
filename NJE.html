Summary of Dec 27 Meeting

I have asked the participants what they would like to study in the following weeks, and they have come up with the following list:

  • Chinese History from Yao to Mao
  • Marxism
  • Mikail Bakunin
  • Russian History
  • North/South Korea
  • Vietnam war
  • Socialism & The Socialist Manifesto
  • League of Nations
  • EU, The Declaration of Human Rights(HR)
  • Human Rights problems in Turkey
  • History of Greece
  • Political theory
  • Medieval Europe
  • History of Islam
  • History of Medicine
  • History of Music
  • European Culture
  • Asian Cultures
  • Mythologies
  • Etruscans
  • Changing Ethnical groups in the Arabian Peninsula
  • Musical Genres
  • Genghiz Khan
  • Ancient writing of Mongolia
A unanimous decision was reached to study the history of religions in the upcoming weeks. We shall begin with the earliest religions. A whatsapp group for research has been created to collaborate on these investigations.
